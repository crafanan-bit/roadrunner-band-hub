A Welcome from Mr. Rafanan

I would like to welcome you to the Tomas Rivera Middle School Music Program for the 2025-2026 school year. My name is Colton Rafanan and I'm proud to serve as the Band Director of this program.

Music is an important component to a student's educational experience. In this program, students can build self-esteem, social skills, and creativity in a safe learning environment. Students from this program, equipped with musical training, learn skills such as working effectively in a group setting, utilizing strong communication skills, and using problem-solving techniques to overcome new concepts.

My goal as a teacher is to guide my students to success through musicianship. This program is built to allow students to take pride in their performances and their musical work. I look forward to working with students and anticipate a year of hard-work, learning, and fun. I encourage parents and guardians to participate in the band volunteer program and watch our future concerts and performances.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me. Thank you for your support!

Grading Policy

Grading Categories

A student's grade is determined by their performance in concerts, assignments, and class participation. Concerts are the primary final assessment.

Grading Scale

  • A (85-100%): Exceeds Proficiency
  • B (70-84%): Meets Proficiency
  • C (55-69%): Approaching Proficiency
  • D (40-54%): Partially Proficient
  • F (0-39%): Below Proficiency

Category Details

Concert Performances (40%)

Graded on in-person group performance. This is the primary final assessment. Points can be lost for tardiness, unexcused absences, forgotten equipment, or disruptiveness. Students are responsible for arranging make-up work.

Assignments (20%)

Submitted on Google Classroom or Make Music. These vary from music history to playing assignments and are completed on the student’s own time. Details will be posted in Google Classroom.

Journal (10%)

Short daily or weekly assignments to review material, pre-teach concepts, analyze music, and for self-reflection. Journals are checked two weeks before the end of each quarter.

Playing/Vocab Tests (30%)

Periodic playing tests to check for understanding, graded on accuracy, technique, and musicality. Vocabulary tests will also be given. Make-ups are by appointment only.

Uniform Requirements

Beginning Band

Concert Season Uniform

  • Shirt: Any White Shirt/Blouse.
  • Pants: Black jeans or slacks.
  • Shoes: Black dress shoes or solid black sneakers.
  • Socks: Solid black, no designs.

Intermediate & Adv.

Concert Season Uniform

  • Shirt: Black Band Polo.
  • Pants: Black jeans or slacks.
  • Shoes: Black dress shoes or solid black sneakers.
  • Socks: Solid black, no designs.

Marching Band

For rehearsals & performances

  • Shirt: Plain Black Shirt or PE Shirt.
  • Shorts: Athletic shorts.
  • Socks: Long, solid black socks.
  • Shoes: Students will be required to purchase black marching shoes. Link to purchase Speedster Shoes
